## PA

- MPA diameter > 29 mm
- RPA > 21 mm
- LPA > 19 mm

## RDPA

- Male < 16
- Female < 15

## Aorta

Aneurysm: 5-4-3

**Normal**

- < 2.5 cm (Upper)
- < 2 cm (Lower)

**Abnormal**

- Ectasia: 2.5 - 3 cm
- Aneurysm: > 3 cm

### Aortic Aneurism

**Dilate**

- Thoracic
    - Ascending > 4 cm
    - Descending > 3.5 cm
- Abdominal
    - `> 3` cm (Aneurism)
    - 2.5 - 3 cm (Ectasia)

**Surgery Candidate**

- AAA: > 5.5 cm (need Sx); > 5 cm (consider Sx)
- TAA:
    - Ascending > 5.5 cm
    - Descending > 6 cm

## Artery

### SMA Syndrome

- Aorto-mesenteric angle < 22 degree (sagital)
- Aorto-mesenteric distance < 8 mm (axial)

## Vein

### IVC

- **Slit-like IVC**: AP diameter `< 9` mm, TV:AP diameter `> 3:1`

### Abdomen

- Splenic v. < 11 mm
- SMV < 12 mm
- Portal v. < 13 mm
